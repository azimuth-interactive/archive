Directions
From New York City:
West Side Highway to George Washington Bridge to Palisades Parkway North. Take Palisades to the end and cross the Bear Mountain Bridge. Make a left onto 9D North. Travel about 4 miles to traffic light. Go straight after the light and the farm is 8/10ths of a mile down on the left side of the road.
From 1-84:
Take Exit 13 (Fishkill) to Route 9 South for 10 miles. Look for Garrison Golf Course on the right. Take the first right past the golf course (Snake Hill Road). Follow to end. The Riverstone entrance is diagonally opposite to the left.
From Route 9:
Take Route 9 north to Garrison, just past Graymoor to light (Route 403). Turn left at light onto Route 403. Follow to end and make a right onto Route 9D. Riverstone is 8/10ths of a mile on the left.
From NJ:

Take Route 9W North to the Bear Mountain Bridge. After bridge, go left onto Route 9D North. Continue to light. Riverstone is 8/10ths of a mile on the left.
