RIVERSTONE
Riverstone is a 20 acre hunter-jumper facility located in Garrison, New York, 60 miles north of New York City in the scenic Hudson Valley.
The farm has 33 box stalls, an attached indoor arena, large outdoor arena, good turnout and an excellent program for adults and juniors who want to ride and show in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere.
Trainer Kim Perlman has run Riverstone since 1994. During that time has worked with numerous junior and adult riders showing at all levels from leadline to the A/O jumpers.
