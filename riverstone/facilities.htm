 Barn
Riverstone's barn has 33 12 x12 box stalls (some are larger). All stalls have rubber mats. There is an indoor heated wash stall as well as an outdoor wash stall, tackroom, office and attached indoor arena.
 Outdoor Ring
The outdoor ring is 200 x 200, with limestone footing. It is regularly dragged and watered and offers excellent footing.
 Indoor Ring
The 200 X 80 indoor arena has stonedust footing that is also regularly dragged and watered. The ring has a heater and a small viewing area.
 Turnout
The farm has six large grass paddocks, two smaller paddocks and a medical paddock.
